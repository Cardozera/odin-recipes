Lasagna

Lasagna, with its layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, and creamy béchamel, is a quintessential Italian delight.

Lasagna, a delectable Italian dish, features layers of pasta, savory meat sauce, and creamy béchamel, creating a culinary masterpiece.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces/800g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 ounces/170g) tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups (475ml) ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups (200g) mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 cup (50g) Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1 egg
  • Fresh parsley (optional, for garnish)

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook Lasagna Noodles:
    1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
    2. Drain and set aside.
  3. Prepare Meat Sauce:
    1. In a large skillet, brown 1 pound (450g) of ground beef over medium heat.
    2. Add finely chopped onion and minced garlic. Cook until the onion is softened.
    3. Stir in a can (28 ounces/800g) of crushed tomatoes, a can (6 ounces/170g) of tomato paste, 1 teaspoon each of dried oregano and dried basil, and salt and pepper to taste.
    4. Simmer the sauce for 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
  4. Prepare Cheese Mixture:
    1. In a bowl, combine 2 cups (475ml) of ricotta cheese, 2 cups (200g) of shredded mozzarella cheese, 1/2 cup (50g) of grated Parmesan cheese, and 1 egg.
  5. Assemble Lasagna:
    1. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce.
    2. Place a layer of cooked lasagna noodles on top.
    3. Add half of the ricotta cheese mixture, spreading it evenly.
    4. Repeat the layers, finishing with a layer of meat sauce on top.
  6. Bake:
    1. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes.
    2. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until the top is bubbly and golden.
  7. Rest and Garnish:
    1. Let the lasagna rest for a few minutes before slicing.
    2. Garnish with fresh parsley if desired.
