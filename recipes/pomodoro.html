Spaghetti al Pomodoro, a Simple Tomato Delight

Spaghetti al Pomodoro is a classic Italian pasta dish celebrated for its simplicity and the vibrant flavors of fresh tomatoes. This dish highlights the essence of ripe tomatoes, garlic, basil, and olive oil, creating a light yet satisfying meal that captures the essence of Italian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 400g (about 14 ounces) spaghetti
  • 4 ripe tomatoes, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) extra-virgin olive oil
  • A handful of fresh basil leaves, torn
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving

Steps

  1. Boil Spaghetti: Cook the spaghetti in a pot of salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve a cup of pasta cooking water before draining.
  2. Sauté Garlic and Tomatoes: In a pan, sauté thinly sliced garlic in olive oil until fragrant. Add diced tomatoes and cook until they break down and form a sauce.
  3. Season and Simmer: Season the tomato sauce with salt and black pepper. Allow it to simmer for a few minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
  4. Toss with Spaghetti: Toss the hot, drained spaghetti in the tomato sauce, ensuring the pasta is well-coated. Add pasta cooking water if needed to reach the desired consistency.
  5. Add Fresh Basil: Stir in torn fresh basil leaves, imparting a burst of herbal freshness to the dish.
  6. Serve: Plate the spaghetti al Pomodoro and serve immediately, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese. Enjoy the simplicity and authentic flavors of this classic Italian pasta.
